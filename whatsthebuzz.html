What's the Buzz?

From our humble beginnings to our ongoing mission, Bumble has strived to benefit our customers and our honeybees. See what Bumble is currently up to!

  • July 2016 - August 2016

    Our Humble Bumble Beginnings

    Bumble begins with the intention to fill the gap in Bakersfield, California's pollination and bee hive rental services. With 22 employees and a mission to help the honeybee population, Bumble's beginnings take root.

  • August 2016 - September 2016

    Bumble Begins to Bud

    Bumble aquires 500 beehives to begin honey harvesting. Focused on cultivating and protecting the local honeybee, the Bumble team reaches out to local farmers and gardeners getting ready for the upcoming pollination season.

  • September 2016 - October 2016

    Bumble Blooms

    Bumble harvests and jars nearly 2 tons of various types of honey from its 500 beehives. The Bumble team is in high gear as we prep for our opening on November 1st, 2016. The Bees' Needs outreachprogram begins to take shape.

  • November 1st, 2016 - Present

    Bumble's in Business

    Bumble opens its doors for the first time on November 1st, 2016. The Bumble website is officially live, taking in sales from all over California and the US. Our beekeepers and Bumble representatives travel to deliver and offer Bumble's beehive rentals to farmers and gardeners all across California.
